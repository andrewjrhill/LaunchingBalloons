Want to see the world from Space?

We can show you how

We are a group of friends dedicated to taking stunning pictures of Earth from space using cameras attached to the bottom of our custom-made weather balloons. We’ll highlight the pros and cons of these endeavors, and show how we’re giving back to the scientific community.

With each launch we add to our technology and improve our process. Here is how we do it.

We started with a lot of trial and error and extensive research. As with any scientific pursuit, there are very serious risks involved with sending a weather balloon into space. We implore you to brush up on our recommended safety tips before persuing this venture any further. We are in no way, shape or form liable or responsible for any injury, damage to property or incidents that may occur as a result of this information. Proceed at your own risk! But remember to have fun out there.

Our group of colleagues continues to grow and to push each consecutive launch one step further. Meet the team.

We constantly strive to improve on our past designs and implement new technologies in our search for optimal results with the least amount of risk.
